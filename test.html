A platform that features recipes with an interactive twist. A look into the kitchens of other users from around the world. An interactive space where cooking secrets can be shared and discussed. The ability to keep up with favourite chefs by choosing to ‘Follow’. An incredible ‘Search’ feature that allows members to filter recipes by taste, type of diet, dish, a single ingredient or language. A personal cookbook that can be accessible by others or kept private. The option to automatically create a shopping list for each recipe which can be modified.
  • Access to detailed recipes shared by other users
  • Option to save other recipes to ‘Favourites’
  • Ability to share recipes with others or simply record recipes that are only visible to you
  • A home page which showcases recipes that fit pre-selected personal preferences
  • Option to automatically create shopping lists for one or multiple recipes at the same time which can be modified
  • Ability to communicate with other members of the community as well as rate and comment posts
  • Share and search for recipes in multiple languages
  • Option to add pictures with step by step instructions to avoid confusion amongst differing language speakers
